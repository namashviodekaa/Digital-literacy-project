
Description:
The infographic was created using Canva to present the concept of digital literacy in a simple and visually engaging way. It highlights key areas such as understanding digital literacy, safe internet practices, and maintaining a professional online presence. The design includes short definitions, icons, and examples to make the content easy to understand for students. It also emphasizes the importance of using digital tools responsibly and protecting personal information while browsing online.
The infographic aims to spread awareness among students about how digital literacy is not just about using technology, but also about using it wisely, safely, and effectively in academic and professional life. One interesting aspect of creating this infographic was selecting the most important points and presenting them in a limited space without overcrowding the design. It was slightly challenging to balance visual appeal with informative content, but it helped improve my creativity and understanding of digital communication.
